# Mira Health Product Context

## Register

Product app and healthcare commerce platform. The interface should serve repeated daily use, not market the brand.

## Audience

Users who want hospital health-check packages selected for their goals, then a long-term AI health dashboard after results arrive. Secondary users include hospital sales/admin teams and referral partners such as doctors, nurses, clinics, and health creators.

## Personality

Quietly capable, reassuring, clinical enough to feel trustworthy, warm enough to feel personal. Commercial surfaces must feel transparent, not pushy.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize quick comprehension over decoration.
- Make the next action obvious: login, answer AI intake, choose package, pay, book, upload results.
- Use health data carefully; never make sensitive information feel casual.
- Keep AI recommendations explainable and secondary to user agency.
- Every health datum should have a date, source, freshness status, and confidence level where possible.
- Referral and hospital settlement logic should be visible enough for operations without cluttering the user purchase flow.

## Core Flows

1. Login with phone OTP or LINE.
2. AI agent collects user context and health goals.
3. Marketplace recommends hospital health-check packages.
4. User buys a package with optional referral attribution.
5. Hospital sales/admin looks up the paid order and schedules booking.
6. After hospital visit, uploaded results become a dated personal health record.
7. AI turns results plus user context into a readable dashboard and future recommendations.
